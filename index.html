About TFC Strength

Built inside Spokane Fitness Center, TFC Strength is a powerlifting club for lifters of all levels. From first-time competitors to national contenders, we train together, lift heavy, and support each other in and out of meets.

What We Do

  • Structured training blocks built for results
  • Meet prep & coaching
  • Access to Spokane's premier gym equipment
  • Community events & lifting meets

Our Mission

To build strong bodies and stronger communities — one lift at a time. Whether you’re pulling 135 or 700, we’ve got your back.
